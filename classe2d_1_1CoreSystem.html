Detailed Description

Manages the initialization and shutdown of core SDL subsystems.

The CoreSystem class is responsible for initializing and shutting down the essential SDL subsystems that are required for the engine to function. This includes systems like SDL timers and events, which are fundamental to the engine's operation.
